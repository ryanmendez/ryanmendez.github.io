a little about me..

I am Ryan Mendez. I have always been interested in technology and film making. Making digital art is a way for me to express my creativity and ideas. Designing digitally is my passion, whether it is designing logos or graphics, I never fail to create the best work I possibly can. Film making has been another passion of mine that has started ever since I was younger. I have experience working in After Effects and Premiere. I see myself in the future working for a major company behind the special effects of movies and films or building my own successful company specializing in digital design for other companies.
